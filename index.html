Let's play Crystals Collector!

How to play:

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four magical crystals, each containing a mystery value. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • If you match your total score to the random number given, you win! But be careful, if your score goes higher than the random number, you lose.
  • Each time the game starts, the crystals will get a new mystery value.
